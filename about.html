Meet Mimi

Mimi, the "Sensuality Sensei" and creator of UNVEILED, is an award-winning movement artist (3 international & national titles) who's empowered pole dancers across 15+ cities worldwide to uncover their sensual truth.

Through her unique teaching methodology, she guides students to infuse sensuality into their movement practice, transforming it from exercise to an expression of self.

When not pole dancing, you can find her inhaling bubble tea, designing clothes, or getting lost in beautiful architecture.
