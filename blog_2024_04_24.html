THE POWER OF AUTHENTICITY

It’s not about “what” you do, it’s about “how” you do it.

This is the philosophy that has guided my teaching and my practice for the last few years.

When you see a live show, which performances are the ones that stick with you for a few hours? Which are the ones that stick with you forever?

The ones that impress me and make me go “wow!” are the ones I’ll talk about for hours. The ones that touch my soul are the ones I’ll talk about forever.

Instagram has conditioned us to react a certain way when we see something cool. We want to save it and try it bc we wanna be cool too. It’s an ego thing.

But when someone can look you in the eyes with complete authenticity - that’s when I feel my soul melt out of my body. That’s the thing you can’t save and replicate.

What’s in the box when it’s empty?

It’s a metaphorical riddle that has plagued me for so long. And while I try to set aside the tricks and “cool things” for the practice of connection and vulnerability, it is a practice that gives me only an inch when I go a mile. There is no roadmap to it. There are no drills you can repeat or progressions someone can teach.

The road to authentic self-expression is uncharted.

How often do you pursue it?
